Binaural beats

A binaural beat is when you hear two tones at different frequencies causing the brain to hear the difference in that frequency.

Binaural beats have a variety of uses, from helping with sleep, focus, or meditations being some of most common.

This works by manipulating the state of brain waves and helping us to regulate. Some people even consider it a form of biohacking.
